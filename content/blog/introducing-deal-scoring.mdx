## What's new

Glyphic now analyses your CRM data and call recordings to automatically assign a score to every open deal. No setup required — scores appear in your deal view as soon as you connect your CRM.

## How it works

The scoring model looks at three signals:

1. **Recency** — how recently the prospect engaged (calls, emails, meetings)
2. **Velocity** — whether engagement is increasing or decreasing over time
3. **Stage fit** — how your activity pattern compares to deals you've won at the same stage

## Getting started

Scores are on by default for all workspaces. Head to **Settings → Deal scoring** to adjust thresholds or disable scoring for specific pipelines.
